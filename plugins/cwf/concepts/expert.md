# Expert

## Definition

Expert is the governance concept that names role-specific quality judgment and advisory authority used by CWF skills and references.

## Ownership Boundaries

- Defines expected expert lenses and what each lens is accountable for.
- Does not replace deterministic gates; experts interpret evidence.

## Operational Rules

- Expert role definitions must map to observable review outputs.
- Expert advice must remain attributable to a named lens.
